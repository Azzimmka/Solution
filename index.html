About Aral Sea

Ladies and gentlemen, Today I want to speak about something very important — the Aral Sea. This sea was once full of life. Now it is almost gone. The land is dry. The air is dirty. The weather is too hot in summer and too cold in winter. People are suffering. But we can change this. We must save water. We must build smart irrigation systems. We must protect our nature. This is not only a problem for Kazakhstan or Uzbekistan. This is a problem for all of us — for Central Asia, for the world. The United Nations says we have only 5 years before a big water crisis begins. But I believe we still have time. If we work together, we can save our future. Let’s act now. Let’s protect our water. Let’s bring life back to the Aral Sea. Thank you.

Mark Zuckerberg is an American computer programmer and businessman. He was born on May 14, 1984, in New York, USA. His full name is Mark Elliot Zuckerberg. Mark started using computers when he was a child. He liked to create games and simple programs. He was very smart and good at coding. In 2004, Mark was a student at Harvard University. There, he made a website called Facebook with his friends. At first, it was only for students, but later it became open to everyone. Facebook became very popular. Many people around the world use it to connect with friends, share photos, and chat. Mark became one of the youngest billionaires in the world. Today, Mark is the CEO of Meta, the company that owns Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. He still works to make social media better. Mark is married to Priscilla Chan, and they have children. He also gives money to help schools and hospitals.
